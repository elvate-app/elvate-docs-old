# Subscribe to a Pair

To subscribe to a Pair, follow the link [https://app.elvate.io/#/pairs](https://app.elvate.io/#/pairs).

Click on your favorite Pair

![Select a Pair](../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot\_20220421\_163028.png)

Then, fill the subscription amount and press the `Subscribe` button

![Subscribe to a Pair](../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot\_20220421\_163120.png)

{% hint style="info" %}
You don't need to have deposit to subscribe to a Pair, but your subscription is considered not eligible if you don't have enough fund.
{% endhint %}

If you are already subscribed, press the `Edit` button instead of `Subscribe`

After this, you will see the selected Pair in the `My Pairs` section.
